## Core Class Features

When you gain this Class, you gain the Aggressive and Aura Class Features.

### Aggressive

When you Hit or Critical Hit on an Action using a Physical Ability, you can spend 5 Blood and take a Personal Cost. If you do, you gain an additional Extra on that Action.

### Aura

You gain a Resource Counter named "Aura". The Counter has a number of slots equal to the sum of your Might, Guile, and Confidence ranks divided by 3. Each slot is worth 1 Aura. Clear all filled slots in this Counter on a Major Milestone.

When you take tthe Hinder or Influence Action, you can fill up to five unfilled Aura slots. For each filled slot, you gain +1B Circumstance on the Check.

Whenever you gain an Extra on a Might, Guile, or Confidence Check, clear up to one filled Aura slot for each Extra.
