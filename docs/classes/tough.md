## Core Class Features

When you gain this Class, you gain the Aggressive and Aura Class Features.

### Aggressive

When you Hit or Critical Hit, you can spend 5 Blood and take a Personal Cost. If you do, you gain an Extra on that Action.

### Aura

You gain a Resource Counter named "Aura". The Counter has a number of slots equal to the sum of your Might, Intellect, and Confidence ranks divided by 3. Each slot is worth 1 Aura. Clear all filled slots in this Counter on a Major Milestone.

When you take an Eliminate, Hinder, Influence, or Traverse Action, you can fill up to five unfilled Aura slots. For each filled slot, you gain +1B Circumstance on the Check.

Whenever you gain an Extra on a Might, Grace, or Endurance Check, clear up to one filled Aura slot.

## Major Class Features

### Deadly Brawler

*Rank 2 Tough.*

Whenever you use an unarmed strike or a blunt implement (such as a club, tire iron, or baseball bat) for Violence, Upgrade your Potency for that Action.
